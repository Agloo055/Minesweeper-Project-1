Minesweeper!

    Intructions:
  • The goal of the game is to reveal all squares that don't have a bomb it.
  • If the square has a number on it, it means there are that many bombs in the surrounding 8 squares.
  • If the square has no number on it, all surrounding squares will also be revealed until number squares are revealed or the border is hit.
  • You may flag squares that may have bombs in them, up to the number of bombs on the board.
  • Press Easy/Intermediate/Hard to change the board.
  • Press Reset to reset the board.
  • Number on at the top-left of the board shows how many flags you have left.
  • Number on at the top-right of the board shows the time.
    The controls:
  • Left Click to reveal a square
  • Press SPACE while mouse is over a square to flag it
